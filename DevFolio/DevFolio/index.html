About me

As a kid, I used to get in trouble for spending too much time organizing my room. I've always loved clean design, though nobody was calling it that at the time. When I got out of school, going into design was a natural next step for me.

I started studying at Chandigarh University, Gharuan, and learned to code. I wasn't that fluent with coding but then I found out that I needed to practice more. So, I spent more time on practising then my skills improved.

I would then show my work to my professors and they praised that along with my positive attitude and ability to taake harsh criticism. Things can get stressful when you get stuck at one place in code and cannot solve the problem, but I always keep calm and carry on. At the end of the day, I want to be known not just for my high-quality design work, but also for being a dependable teammate.

Services

More about what I can do.

Prototyping

I possess a strong proficiency in prototyping, a vital aspect of the UI/UX design process. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating seamless user experiences, I excel in translating design concepts into interactive and functional prototypes. Equipped with expertise in tools like Sketch, Figma, and InVision, I am adept at crafting high-fidelity prototypes that accurately capture the essence of the final design. From low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity clickable mockups, I adapt my prototyping approach to suit project requirements and timelines. Collaborating seamlessly with cross-functional teams, I facilitate user testing sessions and gather valuable feedback to refine and enhance the user experience. Through an iterative design process, I continuously iterate on prototypes, incorporating user feedback and stakeholder requirements to deliver intuitive and user-centric design solutions.

Graphic Design

With a deep passion for visual storytelling and a strong command of design principles, I am a skilled graphic designer. Through my expertise in Adobe Creative Suite and other industry-standard tools, I bring concepts to life by creating captivating visuals, impactful illustrations, and compelling layouts. I have a keen eye for typography and color theory, ensuring cohesive and aesthetically pleasing designs. Whether it's designing logos, marketing materials, or digital assets, I strive to create designs that effectively communicate brand messages and resonate with target audiences. With a meticulous attention to detail and a creative mindset, I consistently deliver visually stunning designs that leave a lasting impression.

User Research

I strongly believe that understanding users is the cornerstone of successful design. As a user research specialist, I am experienced in conducting qualitative and quantitative research methods to uncover deep insights into user behaviors, needs, and motivations. Through user interviews, surveys, and usability testing, I gather valuable data that informs the design process. I excel in synthesizing research findings into actionable recommendations, enabling evidence-based design decisions. By advocating for the user's perspective throughout the design lifecycle, I ensure that the end product meets user expectations and delivers a seamless and delightful user experience.

Branding

I have a passion for helping businesses establish their unique identity and tell their story through effective branding. With a strategic mindset and a creative flair, I specialize in crafting cohesive brand experiences that resonate with target audiences. Through comprehensive market research and competitor analysis, I develop brand strategies that differentiate and position businesses for success. I excel in creating memorable brand assets, including logos, visual identities, and brand guidelines, that accurately reflect the brand's personality and values. By leveraging my expertise in brand messaging and visual storytelling, I ensure that every touchpoint reinforces the brand's essence, fostering brand loyalty and recognition.
